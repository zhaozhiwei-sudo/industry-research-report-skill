# Report Template

## Recommended Page Sections

1. Hero: industry name, report date, tags, conclusion score/rating.
2. Metrics: policy node, demand node, report inventory count, valuation/trading summary.
3. Executive conclusion: allocation conclusion, core stock pool, buying constraints.
4. Key facts: A/B-sourced facts and evidence strength.
5. Industry logic chain: demand root, profit source, valuation switch, disconfirming conditions.
6. Research-report consensus: theme, sources, consensus conclusion, investment use.
7. Demand split: source of demand, nature, evidence, related companies.
8. Price/capacity tracking: price series, supply/demand notes, missing data.
9. Technology roadmap: technology route, stage, value, risk.
10. Company stratification: company, tier, segment, key point, risk.
11. Valuation and trading dashboard: PE, PB, market cap, turnover, turnover rate, ownership, percentile if available.
12. Announcement verification: latest reports, contracts, financing, regulatory notices.
13. Risk triggers and next data to collect.
14. Source and report-name list.

## HTML Guidelines

- Use a real usable report as the first screen, not a landing page.
- Keep cards for repeated items, metrics, and framed tools only.
- Use tables for source lists, valuation dashboards, and company comparisons.
- Keep section headings compact and navigable with anchors.
- Ensure text fits on mobile and desktop.
- Avoid decorative visuals that obscure data.
- Prefer a restrained research-terminal look: dense but readable, with clear hierarchy.

## Source List Columns

- Grade
- Source / institution
- Report or material title
- Date
- Coverage market
- Use in report
- URL

## Multi-Industry Switchboard

For every new or refactored report:

1. Regenerate or update the individual HTML report.
2. Update the switchboard metadata.
3. Update the audit page counts and candidate list.
4. Verify all three pages in browser.

For a single-file suite, embed reports with `srcdoc` or sanitized inline content and preserve:

- Industry switching
- Search
- Audit view
- Source links
- Deep links where feasible
